Collaborative Mail Lists

eLua currently has a single Developper's and User's Discussion Mail List.
You are very welcome to join it at https://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/elua-dev

Our devellopment repository is currently maintained in a Subversion Server.
If you are interested in following daily commitments to the Bleeding Edge trunk development, you can also subscribe to the SVN Activity List at https://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/elua-svn
